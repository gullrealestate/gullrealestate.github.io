## 3. Agency Fee & Liability Policy

### 3.1 About Our Services

We are a real estate agency providing professional guidance, coordination, negotiation support, and deal execution services. **We do not directly sell or rent properties through this website.** Property payments are made separately between the concerned parties. Our charges represent agency fees for services rendered.

### 3.2 Fee Structure

- The agency fee is determined based on the size, type, and value of the transaction.
- Standard agency fees range from **1% to 2%** depending on deal size and complexity.
- Fees cover professional facilitation, coordination, and negotiation support.

### 3.3 Refund Policy

- If a transaction is **cancelled before** a formal written agreement is signed between the parties, and the deal is not concluded, any advance agency fee paid **will be refunded**.
- Once a transaction is **formally concluded** through mutual agreement and signing of documents between the concerned parties, the agency fee is considered **fully earned** for services provided and is **non-refundable**.
- Any dispute, change of mind, or cancellation by either party after conclusion of the agreement shall **not affect** the agency's earned fee.

### 3.4 Post-Deal Liability

> The agency acts solely as a **facilitator**. After the deal is concluded and the agency fee is paid, any subsequent transactions, modifications, disputes, or legal actions are strictly between the parties involved. The agency shall not be held responsible, directly or indirectly, for any matter arising after the completion of the agreement.

### 3.5 Transparency Commitment

We are committed to transparency and fairness in all dealings. If you have any questions regarding our fee structure or liability terms, please feel free to discuss them with us **prior to finalizing** your transaction.

## 4. Professional Standards

### 4.1 Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our services a professional, respectful, and transparent experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender, ethnicity, education level, or socioeconomic status.

### 4.2 Expected Behavior

All interactions between the agency, its agents, and its clients shall be governed by:

- **Honesty**: Providing accurate information about properties, market conditions, and transaction costs at all times.
- **Transparency**: Disclosing all relevant fees, terms, and limitations before any commitment is made.
- **Respect**: Treating all clients, regardless of deal size, with equal professionalism and courtesy.
- **Accountability**: Taking responsibility for our coordination role and promptly addressing any service-related concerns.
- **Confidentiality**: Keeping client information, financial details, and contact data strictly private. Personal data (name, phone, budget) is never shared with analytics services or third parties.
- **Non-Discrimination**: Providing equal service quality to all clients without bias.

### 4.3 Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not tolerated in any capacity:

- Misrepresenting property details, ownership status, or market values
- Pressuring clients into transactions they are not comfortable with
- Sharing client personal information (phone numbers, financial details) with unauthorized parties
- Accepting unofficial payments or side-deals outside the formal agency agreement
- Discriminatory behavior toward clients or colleagues

### 4.4 Data Privacy

- Client data submitted through the website is used **exclusively** for deal execution via WhatsApp.
- The website's analytics system uses a **PII blocklist** — fields like phone, name, email, and WhatsApp numbers are **automatically stripped** before any analytics event is dispatched.
- Form data is stored locally on the user's device for draft recovery and lead tracking only.
- No client data is sold, rented, or shared with third-party marketing services.
- The optional backend lead endpoint (if configured) receives only **sanitized, non-PII metadata** (deal ID, agent, timestamp, intent, property type, location).
